Abdul Sattar Edhi

Angel of Mercy

The Richest Poor Man

Timeline

  • Jan 1, 1928 Abdul Sattar Edhi was born in January 1 , 1928 in Gujarat, British India.
  • Aug 14, 1947 Edhi and his family migrated to Pakistan to a city called Karachi.
  • Sep 22, 1947 Edhi found some marketing to do in a wholesale shop as a peddler.
  • Apr 18, 1951 Abdul Sattar Edhi established a considerable institution which is currently called Edhi Foundation. It includes, for example, hospitals, shelters, and orphanages for vagrants where services are wide spread in Pakistan.
  • Sep 16, 2010 Edhi earned more than 250 international awards like he was rewarded an honorary doctorate by the University of Bedfordshire. Other prizes he received like Ramon Magsaysay Award for Public Service , Lenin Peace Prize , and Paul Harris Fellow from Rotary International.
  • Jul 8, 2016 Depressingly, Abdul Sattar Edhi was diagnosed later on with kidney failure and eventually died on 8 July 2016 at the age of 88.
